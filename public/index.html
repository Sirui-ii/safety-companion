Safety Companion

What it asks

Questions designed for emotional check-ins.

01

How are you feeling right now?

Name the emotion, body feeling, or thought that feels strongest.

02

What happened before the feeling changed?

Identify triggers, stressors, or situations that may have raised the intensity.

03

What would help you stay safe right now?

Choose one immediate action, like moving away from risk, contacting someone, or grounding your body.

04

Who can you contact for support?

Pick one trusted person, crisis line, clinician, or nearby place where you are not alone.

Private by design

No recordings. No diary. Just the call.

Lulu is built for hotline-style self-help. We do not record your calls, save what you say, or build a personal profile from your distress. The counters on this page are anonymous totals only.

Your companion

Lulu is not here to judge, diagnose, or make your life sound easy. She is a soft place to start when you need words and a next step.

Not emergency care

If you might hurt yourself or someone else, call emergency services now. In the U.S. and Canada, call or text 988.

Safety Companion is self-help support, not a therapist, clinician, or emergency service.
